Each cadence task scores against its own timing rule, set the outcome from its dropdown. Grading is now percentage of that quarter's own max delivery score, since Q1 has far more scorable tasks (max 45) than Q2–Q4 (max 12, 9, 12) — a flat point threshold would've made the later quarters nearly impossible to clear on delivery alone. Bonus points from the Wins & Bonus Log split into two tiers: major wins (15+ points — expansion order, before/after win, promoter) count in full, uncapped, so a real win visibly moves the score, even past 100%. Smaller signals (touchpoints, feature ships, feedback) count up to 50% of that quarter's delivery max, so routine activity alone can't inflate a thin quarter to Champion. "Formal renewal proposal delivered" (Month 11) didn't come with a specified point value, it's defaulted to 3/0 to match the other one-time deliverables, flag if that's wrong. Data syncs to GitHub, shared by anyone with a token for this repo.
